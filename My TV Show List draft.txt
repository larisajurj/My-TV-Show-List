My TV Show List draft

General Description:
	this application will help users keep track of their tv shows episodes and add ratings and comments about them

User:
	- can create an account
	- firstly login in order to sync with other devices and than has access to all other features
	- search & add tv show to personal list: "watching now" or "want to watch"
	- can view these lists ordered by genre or personal rating
	- mark episode as watched
	- mark tv show as watched
	- add a rating and a comment for every season/ tv show
	- add a custom tv show
	- every user has a profile with profile photo, description, chosen favourite tv show and favourite quote

Developer:
	- add new user
	- add new tv show
	- delete an account
